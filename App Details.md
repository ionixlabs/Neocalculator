## Clear Title & Description
### Title
NeoCalculator - The Ultimate Neumorphic Multi-Platform Calculator

### Short Description
A sleek, modern Neumorphic calculator for powerful math on all your devices.

### Description
NeoCalculator is a modern, high-performance calculator application built with Flutter, designed to provide a seamless and visually stunning experience across Android, iOS, Windows, macOS, and Web. 

Featuring a sophisticated **Neumorphic UI**, it combines a soft, tactile aesthetic with powerful functionality. Whether you need to perform quick daily calculations or handle complex math expressions, NeoCalculator delivers precision and style.

## App Offerings & Features
- **Modern Neumorphic Design**: A premium, "soft-UI" look that is easy on the eyes and provides a tactile feel.
- **Advanced Calculation Engine**: Support for basic arithmetic and advanced math expressions.
- **Full Calculation History**: Keep track of your previous calculations with ease; never lose a result again.
- **Highly Customizable**: Personalize your experience with dynamic themes, custom fonts, and adjustable UI elements (like button corner radius).
- **Responsive & Cross-Platform**: Optimized for every device, from mobile phones to desktop computers.
- **Smooth Animations**: Integrated Lottie animations and animated transitions for a fluid user experience.
- **Haptic Feedback**: Tactile response for every button press, enhancing the user interaction.
- **Privacy Focused**: No unnecessary permissions required; your data stays on your device.
